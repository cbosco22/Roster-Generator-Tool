# Topic Bank

The master list of individual, tweet/post-sized content concepts. Each of the 5 original "Pitch" graphics gets broken down into its component facts/sub-topics here — the point is to spread one dense graphic's worth of information across many smaller posts instead of dumping it all in one piece, per Chris's direction.

The 6-month calendar (`calendar/2026-H2-content-calendar.md`) is generated by rotating through these lists in order, one per weekday slot, so nothing repeats until a bank cycles back around. Add to a bank at any time — the calendar can be regenerated.

## How to feed in real content

For any 🔴 row, the fastest way to unblock it: send the name/photo/stat/clip whenever you have it (no need to wait for its exact calendar date), and reference the bank item (e.g. "here's the Aviation deep-dive info" or "here's a clip for the hitting-development post"). I'll build the graphic/post and it's ready whenever its slot comes up — or sooner, if it's worth posting ahead of schedule.

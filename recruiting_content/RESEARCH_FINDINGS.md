## 1. Coach Bios — ready to draft (Tuesday "People" stream)

All three on-field coaches have solid, sourced bios. Fills 3 of the "Coach Bio" slots immediately.

**Chuck Ristano — Head Coach**
- Native of Valley Stream, NY. Sacred Heart University '04 (sports management) — 4-year letterwinner, 2x captain, 4x NEC All-Academic. Master's from Liberty ('18).
- Named Navy's head coach June 17, 2023 — 6th head coach in the program's last 85 years. 2026 is his 3rd season.
- Path to Navy: assistant/pitching coach at Sacred Heart (2005), Monmouth (2006–09), Temple (2010), **Notre Dame (2011–22, 12 years)**, Florida State (2023).
- Track record: over a 19-year rise through D1, coached 9 conference award winners, 27 All-Americans, 51 all-conference picks; 41 of his players were MLB-drafted, 9 reached the majors. At Notre Dame: 3 NCAA Tournaments, 1 College World Series appearance.
- Personal: wife Lizzy — 17-year Notre Dame softball assistant coach, 2001 Big East Player of the Year; kids Jane and CJ.
- Source: [navysports.com](https://navysports.com/sports/baseball/roster/coaches/chuck-ristano/2374), [Patriot League](https://patriotleague.org/news/2023/6/17/chuck-ristano-named-head-baseball-coach-at-the-naval-academy.aspx)

**Tim Reilly — Pitching Coach**
- Joined Navy August 2022, 4th season in 2026.
- Rutgers alum — 4-year letterwinner at catcher (2006–09), exercise science degree.
- Coaching path: Rider (2011, catchers/hitters), 6 seasons at alma mater Rutgers, 5 seasons at Lafayette (2018–22).
- Source: [navysports.com](https://navysports.com/sports/baseball/roster/coaches/tim-reilly/2828)

**Adam Pavkovich — Hitters/Infielders Coach**
- Joined Navy July 2023, works with hitters and infielders.
- Played 8 years of pro ball in the LA Angels organization, reached Triple-A, 2 MLB spring training invites (2008, 2009), 708 career minor league games.
- Alabama alum — 3-year letterman at shortstop (2001–03).
- Coaching path: volunteer assistant (2014–15), Notre Dame (2019), Western Kentucky (2020–22, catchers/outfielders + recruiting coordinator).
- Source: [navysports.com](https://navysports.com/sports/baseball/roster/coaches/adam-pavkovich/2401)

**Chris Bosco — Assistant Coach**
- Joined Navy July 2025 (1st season in 2026), works with offense and catchers.
- Prior 5 of the last 6 seasons on the Northeastern staff — helped Northeastern to 2 NCAA Tournament berths, mentored 7 All-America hitters and 3 CAA Players of the Year. Guided the Huskies' base-running attack to 2nd nationally in stolen bases in back-to-back seasons and the nation's longest active winning streak (27 games) in 2025.
- Played at Roger Williams University — 3x all-conference, 2x team captain, 2019 Commonwealth Coast Conference Defensive Player of the Year, All-CCC First Team.
- North Andover, MA native.
- Source: [navysports.com](https://navysports.com/news/2025/7/8/chris-bosco-named-assistant-baseball-coach-at-naval-academy.aspx), [Eagle-Tribune](https://www.eagletribune.com/sports/bosco-moves-on-to-navy-north-andover-native-takes-role-as-top-assistant/article_3578f281-d48e-4d4a-9f83-4114fb48ac75.html)

**Andrew Moritz — Director of Baseball Operations**
- Joined Navy June 2025.
- Pro career: 6 years, 5 seasons in the Atlanta Braves organization after being drafted in the 6th round of the 2018 MLB Draft. Reached Double-A Mississippi; Southern League Player of the Week (Sept. 2022) and MiLB Organizational All-Star (2022).
- College: 3 seasons at UNC-Greensboro under now-Florida State head coach Link Jarrett. 2018 ABCA/Rawlings First Team All-American, D1Baseball.com 2nd Team All-American, Baseball America/Perfect Game 3rd Team All-American, 3x All-Southern Conference, 2018 SoCon Player of the Year. Academics: CoSIDA Academic All-American (1st Team 2017, 2nd Team 2018).
- **Nice program-history callback:** played the 2017 Cape Cod League summer for the Harwich Mariners alongside former Navy ace Noah Song (the "highest-ever Navy MLB draft pick" from §2) — a real, sourced thread connecting current staff to the program's alumni pipeline.
- Coaching path before Navy: Missouri Western (2025, D2, 3 All-MIAA selections in his one year), his alma mater Providence HS in Charlotte, NC.
- 2021 UNC-Greensboro grad, business administration.
- Source: [navysports.com](https://navysports.com/news/2025/6/25/andrew-moritz-named-navy-baseballs-director-of-operations.aspx)

## 4. USNA Structure — unblocks 3 "USNA Life" bank items

Official numbers from usna.edu, confirms it's safe to publish without fabricating structure:

- Brigade of Midshipmen ≈ 4,400 midshipmen, organized into **6 battalions × 6 companies = 36 companies**.
- Each company ≈ 150 midshipmen across all 4 class years, with a shared "wardroom" common area.
- Each company breaks into 4 platoons → 3 squads each (10–14 midshipmen per squad).
- Top student leadership role: **Brigade Commander**, a 1st-class (senior) midshipman selected for leadership performance.
- Source: [usna.edu — Life in the Brigade](https://www.usna.edu/Admissions/Student-Life/Life-in-the-Brigade.php), [CollegeVine](https://www.collegevine.com/faq/132561/how-is-the-naval-academy-brigade-organized)

This directly unblocks bank items #2 ("how the company system works"), and gives real numbers for #7 ("leadership structure") — no longer needs to stay a placeholder.
